About

Welcome to the Bloodaxe Poetry App (alpha-test version)!

The app is currently under construction, but contains some sample content of a few Bloodaxe poets. Feel free to browse this content by returning to the home screen and selecting a browse option.

Content can be browsed by name of Poet and by title of Poem, or by poems featuring Audio or Video recordings. Poets are also sorted in Categories and poems featured in the app have been tagged with Themes to allow you to browse related content. Once you have added some poems to your Favourites list, you can use this option to navigate to them quickly. You can also search for content by clicking the Search icon on the Home screen.

After reading, listening to, or watching a poem, the app currently allows you to share recommendations for a poet via social media and email, add the poet to your favourites list, or tag the poem with themes. You can also change how the poem appears on the screen using the Display option.

This app is being developed for viewing on all devices, but with a priority on mobile touchscreens. At the moment, we recommend using Google Chrome or Safari browser on a tablet computer or larger smartphone for optimum viewing, but we're working on everything else!

In future versions, we hope to improve the look and feel of the app, as well as adding more Bloodaxe poets to the list, but we'd also like to add and improve other features based on the thoughts of poetry readers. Please help us to improve the app by completing this feedback form.
